HOW TO LOOK STUNNING

MANICURE

Our manicure service is designed to give your hands the care and attention they deserve. Our experienced technicians will work with you to determine your desired look. We offer a variety of options, including traditional polish, gel polish, and nail art, all using high-quality products that ensure a long-lasting, beautiful finish. We take the time to clean and shape your nails to perfection, making sure that every cuticle is trimmed, and every nail is buffed to a smooth finish.

PEDICURE

Our pedicure service is designed to give your feet the same level of care that we provide to your hands. We understand that every client's feet are unique, which is why we take the time to analyze and address any concerns that you may have. Our technicians will soak, scrub, and massage your feet to perfection, ensuring that they are left feeling refreshed and revitalized. Our attention to detail ensures that every nail is perfectly shaped and every cuticle is trimmed.

MALE NAILS

At The House of Nails, we believe that every person deserves to look and feel their best, and this includes our male clients. We understand that manicures are not just for women and that men also want to have clean, well-groomed hands. Our manicure service for males is tailored to meet the unique needs of our male clients, with a focus on ensuring that your nails are clean, healthy, and well-maintained.
